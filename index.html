FAQs

How can I book a ticket?

You can book your ticket online through our website or visit one of our ticket offices at the station.

What is Supratours' baggage policy?

Passengers are allowed to carry one large bag (max 20kg) and one carry-on. Additional luggage may incur extra charges.

How can I cancel or change my booking?

You can cancel or modify your booking via our website or by contacting customer service. Please note that charges may apply based on our cancellation policy.

Does Supratours offer any discounts for students or seniors?

Yes, we offer discounts for students, seniors, and frequent travelers. Check our website for the latest offers and eligibility criteria.

Is Wi-Fi available on Supratours buses?

Yes, all our buses are equipped with free Wi-Fi for your convenience.

Are pets allowed on Supratours buses?

Only small pets in carriers are allowed on Supratours buses, and they must travel with their owners at all times.

What should I do if I lose an item on the bus?

If you lose an item, please contact our lost and found department with details about the route and time of travel.

What safety measures are in place on Supratours buses?

Supratours buses are equipped with safety features such as seat belts, fire extinguishers, and first-aid kits. Our drivers are trained to follow strict safety protocols.
